Welcome

Shaping the Next Generation of Educators

The CSAV Future Educators Hub is dedicated to forming compassionate, competent, and creative teachers who will shape the minds of tomorrow. Through a rigorous BEED program grounded in DepEd standards, we prepare our students not just to teach — but to inspire.

Why Choose CSAV

A Department That Supports You

DepEd-Aligned Curriculum

Our BEED program follows the official DepEd standards, ensuring every lesson plan and teaching demonstration meets real classroom requirements.

Guided Practicum Support

From field study to practice teaching, our structured support system helps you navigate every stage of your teaching journey with confidence.

Holistic Teacher Formation

Beyond academics, we develop character, empathy, and classroom leadership — qualities that define an effective elementary educator.
