Welcome to the Department

Where Passion for Teaching Begins

The CSAV Future Educators Hub is home to future educators who believe in the power of great teaching. Here, we don't just train students to deliver lessons — we form professionals who understand the heart of elementary education. Our program combines DepEd-aligned academic training with hands-on practicum experience, ensuring every graduate steps into the classroom confident, capable, and ready to inspire.

Our Mission

To nurture competent, compassionate, and creative elementary educators who are deeply rooted in DepEd standards — ready to foster meaningful learning in every Filipino child they teach.

Our Vision

A community of exceptional elementary educators transforming classrooms across the country — one well-prepared, confident, and compassionate teacher at a time.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Academic Excellence

A curriculum that goes beyond compliance — fostering deep pedagogical knowledge, critical thinking, and a genuine love for the teaching profession.

Compassionate Formation

Developing not just teaching skills but the empathy, patience, and professionalism that define truly great elementary educators.

Community Impact

Preparing teachers who see their vocation as nation-building — equipped to uplift learners, families, and communities through quality education.
